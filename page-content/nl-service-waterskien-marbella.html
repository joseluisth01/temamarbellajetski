Een Klassieke Watersport Geperfectioneerd

Water skiing is the original towsport — and there's still nothing like the rush of slicing through flat Mediterranean water at speed with the sun on your face. Our sessions take place just off Marbella beach, where the sheltered bay provides consistently smooth conditions ideal for both first-timers learning to get up on two skis and seasoned skiers carving hard on a single slalom ski.

Our professional towboat is equipped with a calibrated speedometer and a pylon designed for water ski towing, while our captains know exactly how to pull a skier out of the water cleanly every time. Whether you want a gentle cruise across the bay or an aggressive workout cutting through the boat's wake, we tailor each session to your goals. Twenty minutes of non-stop skiing is surprisingly intense — and incredibly rewarding.

The minimum age is 12 years old. Under-16s must have a parent or guardian present on the boat. We have smaller skis and shorter ropes available for younger or lighter riders.

You should be comfortable in open water, although you'll wear a buoyancy vest at all times. When you fall, the boat returns to you within seconds. You don't need to swim any distance, just float until we pick you up.

Combo means two skis — it's easier to balance and recommended for beginners. Slalom is a single wide ski used for aggressive turns and higher speeds. If you've never water-skied, you'll start on combos and can progress to slalom once you're comfortable.

Water skiing requires relatively calm water. If conditions are too rough for a good session, we'll contact you to reschedule for free or issue a full refund under our weather guarantee. We check marine forecasts every morning.
