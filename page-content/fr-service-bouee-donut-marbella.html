Rebondissez, Tournez et Éclaboussez

The donut ride is ten minutes of non-stop laughter, screaming, and splashing. You and your friends sit on a giant inflatable ring towed behind a powerful speedboat, gripping the handles as the driver sends you bouncing over waves and spinning through sharp turns. It's the kind of ride where everyone ends up soaked, breathless, and immediately wanting to go again.

Our experienced boat drivers read the group — if you want a gentle ride they'll keep it smooth, but if you signal for more speed they'll crank it up until you're flying off the back. The donut seats between two and four riders at a time, making it perfect for families, groups of friends, or stag and hen parties looking for an explosive activity on the water. No skill is needed — just a sense of humour and a willingness to get very, very wet.
