Beheers de Golven in Marbella

There's nothing quite like the feeling of popping up out of the water, locking your feet into a wakeboard, and carving across a glassy wake with the Marbella skyline behind you. Our wakeboarding sessions are run from a purpose-built towboat driven by an experienced captain who knows exactly how to create the perfect wake for your skill level — whether you're standing up for the very first time or throwing aerial tricks.

Each 15-minute session is packed with action. Our captain adjusts the boat speed, rope length, and line tension to match your ability, giving beginners the gentle pull they need to get up and offering advanced riders the speed and wake shape they crave. The warm, sheltered waters off Marbella beach provide flat conditions most mornings, making this one of the best wakeboarding spots on the entire Costa del Sol.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most beginners stand up within the first few attempts. Our experienced boat driver adjusts speed and rope length to give you the best chance of success. If you can snowboard, skateboard, or surf, you'll pick it up even faster — but no prior board experience is needed.

The minimum age for wakeboarding is 10 years old. Children under 16 must have a parent or guardian present on the boat. We have smaller boards and shorter ropes specifically designed for young riders.

You should be comfortable in deep water, but you don't need to be an Olympic swimmer. You'll wear a life jacket or impact vest at all times, which keeps you buoyant. The boat circles back to pick you up within seconds of any fall.

You're welcome to bring your own wakeboard if you prefer. We provide boards, bindings, ropes, handles, and life jackets as standard, so there's no need to bring anything other than swimwear and enthusiasm.

It might sound short, but 20 minutes of continuous wakeboarding is physically demanding — even for fit riders. Most people are ready for a break after a full session. You can always book back-to-back sessions if you want more time on the water.
