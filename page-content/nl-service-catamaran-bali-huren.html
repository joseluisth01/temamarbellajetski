De Ultieme Zee Ervaring

The Bali 4.0 is the flagship of our charter fleet. Built in 2020, this 12.5-metre sailing catamaran combines the stability and space of a twin-hull design with modern luxury and performance. With 4 private cabins, 4 bathrooms, a massive flybridge sun deck, and an open-plan saloon, she offers more living space than many yachts twice her price.

Whether you choose to sail under wind power or cruise under motor, the Bali 4.0 glides smoothly through the Mediterranean waters. Your professional crew handles everything — navigation, anchoring, and refreshments — so you and your guests can focus on swimming in turquoise coves, sunbathing on the flybridge, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Prices shown are for the complete catamaran, not per person.
