Een Ontspannende Dag op het Water

Not every water activity needs to be high-adrenaline. Our pedal boats offer a wonderfully relaxed way to explore the crystal-clear waters off Marbella beach at your own pace. Families love them because even young children can join in, and the gentle pedalling keeps everyone entertained without requiring any special skills or fitness. It's the closest thing to a floating bicycle, and the views from the water are simply gorgeous.

Each boat seats up to four people and is incredibly stable, so there's no tipping or wobbling to worry about. Pedal along the shoreline, stop to swim in the warm shallows, or just drift and soak up the Spanish sunshine. For extra fun, upgrade to a pedal boat with a built-in slide — kids (and plenty of adults) can't resist climbing up and splashing down into the sea over and over again.
