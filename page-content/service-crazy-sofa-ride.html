The Ultimate Sofa Ride in Marbella

Looking for non-stop laughs and heart-pounding excitement? Our crazy sofa ride is one of Marbella's most popular towable watersports. You and your friends sit on an inflatable sofa that's towed behind a powerful speedboat — bouncing, spinning, and flying across the waves at exhilarating speeds. It's 10 minutes of pure, unforgettable fun.

Perfect for groups of 2 to 4 riders, the crazy sofa is suitable for adventurers aged 6 and up. No experience is necessary — just a willingness to get wet and have the time of your life. Our experienced boat drivers know exactly how to deliver maximum thrills while keeping everyone safe.
