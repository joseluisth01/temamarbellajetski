The Perfect Introduction to Jet Skiing

Our closed circuit jet ski experience is the ideal way to enjoy the adrenaline of jet skiing without venturing into open waters. Set within a secure, buoyed area just off Marbella's stunning coastline, you'll ride a powerful 2024–2025 Yamaha VX (TR-1 HO, 1049 cc, ~125 hp) under the watchful eye of our professional instructors.

Whether it's your first time on the water or you're looking for a quick blast of fun, this activity delivers pure excitement in a controlled environment. The circuit features 4 buoys within a GPS-monitored perimeter, with a maximum of 4 jet skis riding simultaneously to ensure plenty of space.

Every session begins with a comprehensive safety briefing where our team walks you through the controls, hand signals, and riding techniques. We provide all the gear — including a life jacket and wetsuit if needed — so all you have to bring is your sense of adventure.

Each jet ski can carry 2 people (1 driver + 1 passenger) and you can swap driver during the session — same price per jet ski.
All jet skis are GPS-tracked via OtoTrak in real time. Our operations centre monitors speed, position, and perimeter at all times.

How It Works

Arrive & Check In

Find us at Playa de las Dunas, Marbella. Check in at our beachfront station 15 minutes before your session.

Safety Briefing

Our qualified instructors explain controls, hand signals, circuit rules, and riding technique. Takes about 5 minutes.

Gear Up

Put on your CE-approved life jacket (and wetsuit if desired). All safety gear is provided — just bring swimwear.

Ride!

Hit the water and experience the thrill! Open up the throttle, practise turns, and feel the Mediterranean spray.

What's Included

Full Safety Briefing

Every rider receives a thorough on-shore briefing covering controls, signals, and riding technique before entering the water.

Life Jacket Provided

CE-approved life jackets in all sizes are supplied and must be worn throughout your ride for maximum safety.

GPS Tracked (OtoTrak)

Every jet ski is live-tracked via the OtoTrak GPS system. Our operations centre monitors speed, position, and perimeter in real time.

Instructor Supervised

Qualified maritime instructors watch from alongside the circuit on rescue craft, ready to assist immediately if needed.

Secure Riding Area

The buoyed-off circuit keeps jet skis separated from swimmers, boats, and open-water traffic for total peace of mind.

No Boat Licence Needed

Because you ride within a supervised circuit, Spanish maritime law does not require a boating licence. Anyone aged 16+ can drive!
