Le Luxe Redéfini

The Azimut 39 Fly is the crown jewel of our fleet. At 12.3 metres, this Italian-crafted flybridge yacht delivers an experience far beyond ordinary boat charters. Two spacious decks, a fully equipped cabin below, and the signature flybridge sun deck give you and your guests endless room to relax, socialise, and take in the breathtaking Marbella coastline.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, hosting a hen or stag party, or simply treating your group to an unforgettable day at sea, the Azimut 39 Fly delivers in style. Your professional skipper will navigate to the best spots along the coast while you enjoy Bluetooth music, complimentary drinks, and the kind of views that only a flybridge yacht can provide.
