De Glamour van Puerto Banús vanaf het Water

Puerto Banús is synonymous with luxury — superyachts, designer boutiques, and world-class restaurants line its famous harbour. Now imagine approaching it aboard a jet ski, weaving along the sparkling Mediterranean coast with the Sierra Blanca mountains behind you. This guided tour takes you on a thrilling ride from Marbella beach westward to the iconic marina, offering a vantage point usually reserved for those aboard multi-million-euro yachts.

As you ride, your guide points out notable vessels, oceanfront mansions, and hidden stretches of sand that only locals know. The journey itself is exhilarating — open-water cruising mixed with slower sections where the group takes in the views and snaps photos. It's the perfect marriage of sightseeing and adrenaline, wrapped in the unmistakable glamour that only Puerto Banús can deliver.
