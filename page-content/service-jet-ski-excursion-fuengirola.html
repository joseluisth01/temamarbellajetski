Explore the Coast by Jet Ski

This isn't just any ordinary excursion. It's an epic hour-long journey that takes you from Marbella's exclusive coastline to the vibrant harbour of Fuengirola, cruising through some of the most spectacular Mediterranean scenery you'll ever witness.

This adventure gives you exactly what you're looking for: proper time to switch off, feel the genuine freedom of the open sea, and live an experience you'll be talking about for years to come. It's not simply about riding a jet ski — it's about discovering the Costa del Sol from a completely fresh perspective. Available in one-hour and two-hour formats, the excursion is suited to riders who want more than just a quick spin.

What You'll Experience

Epic Departure from Marbella

Launch from our beach base with stunning views of the coastline and, on clear days, the Rock of Gibraltar in the distance.

Coastal Cruising Past Beaches

Navigate along unspoilt stretches of the Costa del Sol, passing exclusive villas and charming coastal villages most tourists never see.

Marine Wildlife Sightings

Keep your eyes open for dolphins, sea turtles and other marine life that frequent these crystal-clear Mediterranean waters.

Arrival at Fuengirola Harbour

The triumphant arrival at Fuengirola's vibrant harbour — a perfect moment for celebration photos before the return journey.

Free Time for Refreshments

Time to relax, grab refreshments and capture incredible photos of the coast and your group before heading back to Marbella.
