De Meest Hilarische Watersport van Marbella

Think you've tried every watersport in Marbella? Think again. The water bull is a giant inflatable mechanical bull that's towed behind our speedboat on the open sea. Your mission: hold on as long as you can while the waves and the driver's sharp turns try to throw you off. Spoiler — almost nobody stays on. And that's exactly what makes it so funny.

Perfect for birthday parties, stag and hen dos, corporate team-building, or just an afternoon of pure silliness, the water bull delivers guaranteed laughs for riders and spectators alike. Suitable for ages 8 and up, it's a 10-minute experience you'll be talking about for years.
