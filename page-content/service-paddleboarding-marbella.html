Discover Marbella From the Water

Stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) is the perfect way to experience Marbella's beautiful coastline. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned paddler, our premium boards and calm morning waters make for an unforgettable session. Paddle along the shore, discover hidden coves, and enjoy breathtaking views of the Sierra Blanca mountains rising behind the town.

Paddleboarding combines relaxation with a full-body workout — it's a fantastic way to stay active while on holiday. Our sheltered launch spot provides calm, flat water ideal for beginners, while more confident paddlers can venture further along the coast. It's also one of the most eco-friendly ways to enjoy the Mediterranean.
