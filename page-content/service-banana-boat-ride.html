Marbella's Favourite Group Activity

The banana boat is the ultimate group watersport experience on the Costa del Sol. Straddle the giant inflatable banana as it's towed behind our speedboat, weaving through the waves at exciting speeds. Try to stay on as the driver takes sharp turns — or enjoy the splash when you don't! It's the perfect icebreaker for hen parties, birthday celebrations, or a day out with friends.

With space for 4 to 8 riders and a minimum age of just 4 years, the banana boat is one of our most family-friendly and budget-friendly watersport activities. At only €20 per person for 15 minutes of non-stop action, it's unbeatable value for laughs and memories in Marbella.
