A Relaxing Day on the Water

Not every water activity needs to be high-adrenaline. Our pedal boats offer a wonderfully relaxed way to explore the crystal-clear waters off Marbella beach at your own pace. Families love them because even young children can join in, and the gentle pedalling keeps everyone entertained without requiring any special skills or fitness. It's the closest thing to a floating bicycle, and the views from the water are simply gorgeous.

Each boat seats up to five people and is incredibly stable, so there's no tipping or wobbling to worry about. Pedal along the shoreline, stop to swim in the warm shallows, or just drift and soak up the Spanish sunshine. For extra fun, upgrade to a pedal boat with a built-in slide — kids (and plenty of adults) can't resist climbing up and splashing down into the sea over and over again.

No Licence Needed

Pedal boats require no boating licence or prior experience. If you can ride a bicycle, you can steer a pedal boat.

Explore the Coastline

Cruise along the Marbella shoreline and discover hidden coves, rocky outcrops, and crystal-clear swimming spots.

Slide Option Available

Upgrade to the slide model and add an extra dimension of fun — climb up, slide down, and splash into the sea on repeat.

Stable Design

Our catamaran-hull pedal boats are extremely stable, even in light chop. You won't tip over, even with excited kids on board.

Fits 5 People

Each pedal boat seats five comfortably with two pedalling stations. Share the workload or let the kids ride as passengers.
