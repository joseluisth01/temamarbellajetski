The Racing Journey

From growing up on the Mediterranean waves to conquering the Spanish National Championships

1998

Family Legacy Begins

The Stiers family establishes STIERS E HIJOS S.L. in Marbella, laying the foundation for what would become the Costa del Sol's premier water sports destination.

2000s

Growing Up on the Water

Young pilots in our team spend their childhood on jet skis, learning every nuance of the Mediterranean. Natural talent meets relentless practice.

2020

Quality Certification

Marbella JetSki achieves ISO 9001:2015 & 14001:2015 certification, proving commitment to quality and environmental excellence.

2023

First Year Competing

Our team enters their first competitive season with the RFEM, earning a 3rd place finish — an impressive debut that signals the champions to come.

2024

National Champion — 1st Place

National Champion with 4 gold medals at the Campeonato de España and Copa del Rey. Our pilots claim 1st Place in Offshore, Circuit, and Rallyjet disciplines with 7 total medals.

2025

Andalusia Champion + National Podium

Campeón de Andalucía 2025 and 3rd Overall National — our team continues to dominate at regional level while competing at the top of the national stage.

NOW

Training the Next Generation

Today, our team combines championship expertise with 25+ years of experience to offer world-class training and unforgettable water experiences.

The Machine

RXP-X 300 Modified

Our competition machine — professionally tuned for championship-level performance.

ECU Stage 2 Remap

Custom engine management calibration removing factory limiters for maximum output across the full RPM range.

1630cc Supercharged — ~350 HP

Rotax 1630 ACE with upgraded supercharger. Approximate output ~350 HP (configuration-dependent). 0–100 km/h in approximately 3 seconds.

Riva Racing Sponsons

Extended rear sponsons for aggressive cornering grip and stability at competition speeds through tight buoy turns.

Riva Racing Intake & Impeller

High-flow intake grate and performance impeller for maximum thrust and acceleration out of every turn.

Riva Racing Handlebar

Extended and reinforced handlebar for superior leverage and control during high-speed manoeuvres and standing riding.

Competition Anti-Slip Seat

Race-spec anti-slip seat cover and deck pads for maximum rider connection through rough water and hard cornering.

Performance figures are approximate and depend on specific configuration, conditions, and rider weight. Modifications are for our professional competition machine.
