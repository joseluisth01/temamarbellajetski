Italian Craftsmanship Meets the Mediterranean

The Cranchi Endurance 39 is the crown jewel of our charter fleet. Built in Italy with meticulous attention to detail, this 39-foot motor yacht combines striking design with exceptional comfort. The generous flybridge offers panoramic views as you cruise past Puerto Banús towards Estepona or east along the coast to Cabopino. Below deck, a fully equipped galley kitchen, spacious cabin, and elegant bathroom provide all the comforts of a floating apartment.

Whether you're hosting a corporate event, celebrating an anniversary, or treating your family to a truly special day out, the Cranchi delivers an experience that rivals any 5-star venue — but with the Mediterranean Sea as your backdrop. Snacks are available on board, and you can add a jet ski to your charter from just €200 for guests who want extra adrenaline between swimming stops.

Prices shown are for the complete yacht, not per person.
